Rick C-137

The Great Intoxicator

Here's a timeline of Rick's life:

  • 2013 - First appearance in Rick and Morty pilot episode, where Rick drags Morty on a chaotic adventure involving Mega Seeds and interdimensional customs.
  • 2013 - Coined catchphrase “Wubba Lubba Dub Dub!”, a phrase that quickly became iconic and was later revealed to mean “I am in great pain, please help me.”
  • 2014 - Revealed origin from Dimension C-137, giving fans their first real glimpse into Rick’s multiversal background and emotional baggage.
  • 2015 - Attended his first wedding—Birdperson’s—only for it to end in betrayal, bloodshed, and a full-on battle against the Galactic Federation.
  • 2017 - Turned himself into a pickle to avoid family therapy, leading to the legendary “Pickle Rick” episode and a brutal sewer fight with a Russian exterminator.
  • 2018 - Took a rare vacation to Atlantis (offscreen), casually mentioning it while brushing off questions about an epic Citadel of Ricks story.
  • 2020 - Battles the Galactic Federation again, demonstrating his unmatched intellect and ruthlessness in the episode “Star Mort Rickturn of the Jerri.”
  • 2021 - Made an incest baby with Morty’s sister (Summer’s egg and Morty’s sperm), resulting in a massive spacefaring being named “Naruto.”
  • 2024 - Killed his greatest enemy—himself from another dimension—confronting his darker nature in a twisted act of vengeance and self-awareness.
